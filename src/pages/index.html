MBG Landscaping

Michael Gilligan has owned MBG Landscaping in Scituate, MA for over 30 years. He has a true passion for landscape design, renovations and stonescapes. He works closely with clients to help bring their vision to life. Over the years he has honed his skills and prides himself on providing each of his clients a design that is unique to them & their landscape.

In addition to landscape design & management, he has fine skills in stonescapes and creating beautiful outdoor spaces that become an oasis for his clients. He also has a passion for building & maintaining stonewalls that are a nod to our richly historical New England landscape.

Through his work he highlights and respects the beauty of South Shore's natural environment. It has always been deeply important for him to build strong client relationships through trust and integrity.
