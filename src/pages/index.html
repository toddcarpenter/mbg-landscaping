About MBG

My name is Michael Gilligan. I have owned my own landscape business in Scituate, Ma for over 30 years. I have a true passion for landscape design, renovations and stonescapes. I work closely with my clients to help bring their dream or vision to fruition. Over the years I have honed my skills and pride myself in providing each of my clients with creating a design concept that's truly unique to them.

The past few years I have expanded my business and out more time and focus into building stonescapes and beautiful outdoor spaces that become an oasis for my clients. I also have a passion for building stonewalls that are a nod to our richly historical New England landscape. Through my work I have tried to highlight and respect the beauty of our natural environment. It has always been deeply important to me to build a strong client relationship through trust and integrity.
